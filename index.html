Research Study: Personalized Support for Physical Activity

We are interested in understanding how personalized messages generated by large language models (LLMs) and contextual multi-armed bandits (cMABs) might support physical activity through behavior change techniques (BCTs).

What is the study about?

The study is divided into four sections:

  • Section 1: Pre-study questionnaires collecting data for personalized intervention strategies.
  • Section 2: Two questionnaires sent daily for 28 days: one with motivational messages at your chosen time, one at the end of the day asking if you were physically active. Includes a 5-minute online check-in meeting after 14 days.
  • Section 3: A concluding questionnaire on your overall experience.
  • Optional Section 4: An online interview (limited places) with extra compensation.

In total, the study requires no more than 4 hours (or 5 hours with the optional interview) spread across 30 days.

Compensation

Participants will receive Amazon gift vouchers via email after the study (mid-November). Compensation depends on completion:

  • Section 1 + 14 days of Section 2 (incl. check-in): 24 CAD (~2 hrs)
  • Section 1 + full Section 2: 38 CAD (~3h 10m)
  • Section 1 + Section 2 + Section 3: 48 CAD (~4 hrs)
  • Section 1 + Section 2 + Section 4 (interview): 60 CAD (~5 hrs)
  • Partial participation (only pre-study questionnaire/dropout before check-in): No compensation

Withdrawal & Rights

Your involvement is entirely optional. You may withdraw at any time without penalty. By signing up, you confirm you are 18+, agree to receive study-related messages, and understand how your data will be used (see Data Protection).

Data Protection & Security

How we handle your data.

Data collected

  • Pre-study questionnaire responses
  • Daily questionnaires (motivational + physical activity check-in)
  • Check-in meeting notes (no recording)
  • Conclusion questionnaire responses
  • Optional interview transcript (if selected)
  • Email address for communication & compensation

Use of data

  • LLM-generated personalized messages use your responses for tailoring
  • Research data is analyzed for scientific publication and conferences
  • No identifying information will be disclosed

Storage & security

  • Stored on secure University of Toronto servers
  • Personal identifiers separated from research data
  • Retained for [X years], then securely deleted

Participant rights

  • Participation is voluntary
  • You may withdraw at any time
  • GDPR rights apply if you are in Europe
