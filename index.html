Research Study: Personalized Support for Physical Activity

We are investigating how personalized messages generated by large language models (LLMs) and contextual multi-armed bandits (cMABs) can support physical activity through behavior change techniques (BCTs).

What is the study about?

This research study consists of four sections:

  • Section 1: Pre-study questionnaires collecting data for personalized intervention strategies
  • Section 2: One brief daily questionnaire for 28 days with motivational messages at your chosen time.
  • Section 3: A concluding questionnaire about your overall experience
  • Optional Section 4: An online interview (limited places available) with additional compensation

The total time commitment is approximately 4 hours (or 5 hours with the optional interview) spread across 30 days.

Compensation

Participants will receive Amazon gift vouchers via email after the study (mid-November through December). Compensation is based on completion level:

  • Section 1 + 14 days of Section 2: $36 CAD (~2 hours)
  • Section 1 + complete Section 2: $57 CAD (~3h 10m)
  • Section 1 + Section 2 + Section 3: $72 CAD (~4 hours)
  • Complete study + Section 4 (interview): $90 CAD (~5 hours)
  • Partial participation (pre-study questionnaire only): No compensation

Withdrawal & Rights

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without penalty. By signing up, you confirm that you are 18 or older, agree to receive study-related messages, and understand how your data will be used (see Data Protection section below).

Data Protection & Security

Information about how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Data We Collect

  • Pre-study questionnaire responses (demographics, preferences, baseline measures)
  • Daily questionnaire responses (motivational message interactions and physical activity reports)
  • Final questionnaire responses about your study experience
  • Optional interview transcripts (for selected participants only)
  • Email address for study communication and compensation delivery

How We Use Your Data

  • Generate personalized motivational messages using AI/ML algorithms
  • Conduct research analysis for scientific publications and conference presentations
  • Improve understanding of digital health interventions
  • All published results will be anonymized with no identifying information disclosed

Data Storage & Security

  • All data stored on secure, encrypted University of Toronto servers
  • Personal identifiers kept separate from research data
  • Access limited to authorized research team members only
  • Data retained for 7 years per university policy, then securely deleted
  • Regular security audits and compliance monitoring

Your Rights as a Participant

  • Participation is completely voluntary
  • Right to withdraw at any time without penalty
  • Right to request deletion of your data (subject to research requirements)
  • GDPR rights apply for European participants
  • Right to file complaints with relevant data protection authorities
